# Periodic Table Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Periodic Table is an educational reference app for browsing chemical elements.

## Data collection

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information.

Element data is bundled with the app. When you open an element detail page, the app may request image metadata from Wikipedia and load an available image from Wikimedia-hosted servers. Those requests are made directly from your device to Wikipedia/Wikimedia services, and those services may receive standard network information such as your IP address and user agent as part of serving the request.

The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, push notifications, location services, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library access.

## Third-party services

The app uses Wikipedia and Wikimedia-hosted resources for optional element images. Their own privacy practices apply to requests served by those services.
